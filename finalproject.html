A8: Final Project
For my final project designed and printed ring splints. Plan details are here.

I started by first working on designs that added small embellishments to the base design, which I didn't want to change too much so it wouldn't lose the angle. Then I created a new base from it that was solid and not two rings, and used that to explore a few more ideas.
There were a couple failed designs that broke while finishing, so they did not have adequate strength to be worn. Others were revealed in Cura to be fails due to lines being too small to print properly.

Rhino and grasshopper were used to make the designs. It wasn't until halfway through that I figured out how to correctly use Bend and CageEdit which helped significantly.
Designs were printed, sanded, and polished. I used nail polish for paint and glued on some 2mm rhinestones.

Cost to make in Shapeways: $40 each made with stainless steel. A strong metal is needed but I'm allergic to most metals so stainless steel is the best option that Shapeways has. My ideal material would be titanium.

Future plans: Some of these designs came out a bit messy since the small parts were hard to sand around. So I'd want to revise them to make finishing easier or get a tool to get into the small places. I also want to do more designs like #7, with an oragnic look that extends up the finger.
